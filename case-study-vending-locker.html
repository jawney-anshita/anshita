Vending App UI

Project Overview

UI for a smart vending machine and locker system aimed at providing users with a seamless, self-service experience. The interface needed to balance ease of use, speed, and clarity, ensuring users could quickly purchase products and access lockers without confusion.

Problem Statement

Traditional vending machines and locker systems often suffer from clunky interfaces, small displays, and poor navigation, leading to user frustration.

Target Audience

  • General Users: Office workers, students, and commuters using vending machines for snacks, beverages, or essentials.
  • Locker Users: Customers who use lockers for package pickups, returns, or temporary storage.

Research & Insights

I gathered insights from business analysts and competitor analysis of existing vending and locker systems.

  • Users prefer touch-based, large-button UIs due to varied screen interaction environments.
  • Clear progress indicators reduce confusion in multi-step flows (e.g., payment + retrieval).
  • Users often need help prompts during locker access.

Challenge: Accessibility across different age groups.

Decision: Used large touch zones, bold colors, and voice guidance options.
