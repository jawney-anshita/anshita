Project Overview

I designed a SaaS Dashboard for company aimed at helping users monitor key metrics, manage workflows, and take quick actions through a clean and intuitive interface.

The primary goal was to deliver a data-rich yet simple-to-use dashboard that reduces complexity, improves productivity, and provides clear insights at a glance.

Problem Statement

The challenge was to design a scalable, modular dashboard UI that balances data visualization with usability.

Target Audience

  • Primary: Business professionals, managers, or teams using the SaaS product daily.
  • Secondary: Stakeholders and executives who need high-level insights quickly.

Key Challenges & Design Decisions

  • Challenge: Too much data risked overwhelming users.
  • Decision: Implemented progressive disclosure‚Äîsummaries upfront, details on click.
  • Challenge: Maintaining readability of charts.
  • Decision: Used color-coded indicators + hover tooltips for extra info.
  • Challenge: Different needs of managers vs. executives.
  • Decision: Designed role-based views‚Äîdetailed dashboards for managers, simplified high-level summaries for executives.
